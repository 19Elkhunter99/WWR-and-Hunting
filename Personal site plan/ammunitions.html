FAQ

Can students of any legal hunting age shoot semi-automatic rifles in a hunter education program course or other training courses?

Yes, students of any legal hunting age can indeed shoot semi-automatic rifles in a hunter education program course.

Can hunters use a semi-automatic rifle for hunting?

Hunters may use a semi-automatic rifle for hunting game that may be taken with a rifle.

Can I hunt on other State, County or Town lands?

While there is an allowance for possession of firearms when lawfully engaged in hunting activity, the new laws do not create an obligation for lands to be open to hunting.
