How do you tell the difference between private or public property?

Public property is owned collectively by the state or community for use, while private property is owned by individuals. While they might seem self-explanatory, the differences between public and private property are significant and have profound implications for individuals, communities, and nations.

How do you know when you crossed into private property?

You will see a sign that says private and you will possibly see a fence. If you want to go hunting in private property, you will have to get permission from the property owner. There is a fine for trespassing into private property. Make sure you read the rules and regulations.
